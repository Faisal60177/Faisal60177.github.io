Privacy Policy — Siratal Mustaqeem

Effective Date: June 1, 2025  |  Last Updated: June 1, 2025

📋 Overview

HealthFit Life ("we", "our", or "us") is developed by Siratal Mustaqeem. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app. We are fully committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent.

✅ We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with any third party.

🏃 Activity & Step Tracking Data

HealthFit Life uses your device's sensors to track physical activity including:

  • Daily step count via device pedometer/accelerometer
  • Physical activity recognition (walking, running, resting)
  • Daily and weekly activity summaries
✅ Activity data is stored only on your device.
✅ This data is never uploaded to any external server.
✅ Never shared with any third party.

⚖️ Health Data (Weight, Calories, BMI)

HealthFit Life allows you to manually enter personal health information such as:

  • Body weight and height
  • BMI (Body Mass Index) calculations
  • Calorie intake and burn tracking
  • Personal fitness goals
✅ All health data is stored locally on your device only.
✅ We do not upload, sync, or transmit this data anywhere.
✅ Only you can see your health data.

🔒 Data We Do NOT Collect

HealthFit Life does not collect:

  • Personal identification (name, email, phone number)
  • Location or GPS data
  • Camera or microphone data
  • Contacts or social data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Any data transmitted to external servers

🔑 Permissions Used

  • ACTIVITY_RECOGNITION — To count steps and detect physical activity
  • BODY_SENSORS — To access device pedometer for step tracking

You can revoke any permission anytime via your device Settings → Apps → HealthFit Life → Permissions.

👶 Children's Privacy

HealthFit Life does not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. Since all data stays on your device and nothing is transmitted externally, the app is safe for all ages.

🔄 Changes to This Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, the "Last Updated" date above will be revised. Continued use of the app after any changes means you accept the updated policy. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
