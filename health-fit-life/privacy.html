📋 Overview

HealthFit Life ("we", "our", or "us") is developed by Siratal Mustaqeem. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, retain, and delete your information when you use our app. We are fully committed to your privacy and transparency.

✅ We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with any third party.

🏃 Activity & Step Tracking Data

HealthFit Life uses your device's sensors to track physical activity including:

  • Daily step count via device pedometer/accelerometer
  • Physical activity recognition (walking, running, resting)
  • Daily and weekly activity summaries
✅ Activity data is stored only on your device.
✅ This data is never uploaded to any external server.
✅ Never shared with any third party.

⚖️ Health Data (Weight, Calories, BMI)

HealthFit Life allows you to manually enter personal health information such as:

  • Body weight and height
  • BMI (Body Mass Index) calculations
  • Calorie intake and burn tracking
  • Personal fitness goals
✅ All health data is stored locally on your device only.
✅ We do not upload, sync, or transmit this data anywhere.
✅ Only you can see your health data.

🔒 Data We Do NOT Collect

HealthFit Life does not collect:

  • Personal identification (name, email, phone number)
  • Location or GPS data
  • Camera or microphone data
  • Contacts or social data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Payment or financial information
  • Any data transmitted to external servers

🗃️ Data Retention

Since HealthFit Life stores all data locally on your device only:

  • Step & activity data — stored on your device until you uninstall the app or clear app data
  • Health data (weight, BMI, calories) — stored on your device until you manually delete it inside the app or uninstall
  • No server data — we do not store any of your data on external servers, so there is nothing retained on our end
  • After uninstall — all locally stored data is permanently and immediately deleted
✅ All your data lives only on your device.
✅ We hold zero data on any server or cloud.
✅ No data is retained after uninstall.

🗑️ How to Delete Your Data

You have full control over your data at all times. Here is how to delete it:

Option 1 — Delete specific entries inside the app:

  • Open HealthFit Life → go to the relevant section (weight log, activity history, goals etc.)
  • Swipe or tap delete on any entry
  • The entry is immediately and permanently removed from your device

Option 2 — Clear all app data (phone settings):

  • Go to your phone Settings → Apps → HealthFit Life → Storage
  • Tap "Clear Data"
  • This permanently removes everything stored by the app on your device

Option 3 — Uninstall the app:

  • Uninstalling HealthFit Life removes all locally stored data permanently from your device

Option 4 — Request via email:

  • Email us at siratalmustaqeem60177@gmail.com
  • Subject: "Data Deletion Request – HealthFit Life"
  • We will confirm deletion within 30 days

🔑 Permissions Used

  • ACTIVITY_RECOGNITION — To count steps and detect physical activity
  • BODY_SENSORS — To access device pedometer for step tracking
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE — To track steps continuously while app runs in background

Revoke any permission anytime via your device Settings → Apps → HealthFit Life → Permissions.

HealthFit Life does not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. Since all data stays on your device and nothing is transmitted externally, the app is safe for all ages.
