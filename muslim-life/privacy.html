Privacy Policy — Siratal Mustaqeem

Effective Date: June 1, 2025  |  Last Updated: June 1, 2025

📋 Overview

Muslim Life ("we", "our", or "us") is developed by Siratal Mustaqeem. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being fully transparent.

✅ We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties.

📍 Location Data

Muslim Life requests access to your device's location for the following purposes only:

  • Calculating accurate Islamic Prayer Times based on your position
  • Determining the Qibla direction (direction of Mecca)
✅ Location data is used only on your device in real time.
✅ We do not store, upload, or transmit your location to any server.
✅ Location is never shared with any third party.

🔒 Data We Do NOT Collect

Muslim Life does not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone)
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Camera, microphone, or contacts data
  • Any data stored on external servers

👶 Children's Privacy

Muslim Life is suitable for all ages including children. We do not knowingly collect any personal data from children under 13. Since no personal data is collected or stored, the app is safe for family use.

🔑 Permissions Used

  • ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION — For precise prayer times and Qibla direction
  • ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION — Fallback location for prayer time calculation

You can revoke location permission anytime from your device Settings → Apps → Muslim Life → Permissions.

🔄 Changes to This Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, we will revise the "Last Updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
