Privacy Policy — Siratal Mustaqeem

Effective Date: June 1, 2025  |  Last Updated: June 1, 2025

📋 Overview

Muslim Life ("we", "our", or "us") is developed by Siratal Mustaqeem. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app. We are fully committed to your privacy and transparency.

✅ We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with any third party.

👤 Account & Authentication

Muslim Life offers optional account registration to enable data sync features. When you create an account we collect:

  • Email address — used for login and account recovery
  • Password — stored securely via Firebase Authentication (hashed, never plain text)
  • User ID — a unique identifier generated by Firebase
✅ Authentication is handled by Google Firebase — industry standard security.
✅ All data is encrypted in transit via HTTPS/TLS.
✅ Passwords are never stored in plain text.

🔖 Favorites & Bookmarks Sync

If you are logged in, Muslim Life syncs the following data to your account via Firebase Firestore:

  • Your saved Duas (favorites)
  • Your bookmarked Adhkar
  • Your app preferences (language, display settings)
✅ This data is linked only to your account.
✅ Only you can access your synced data.
✅ This data is deleted when you delete your account.

📍 Location Data

Muslim Life requests location access only for:

  • Calculating accurate Islamic Prayer Times based on your position
  • Determining the Qibla direction (direction of Mecca)
✅ Location is used only on your device in real time.
✅ Location is never uploaded to any server.
✅ Location is never shared with any third party.

🔒 Data We Do NOT Collect

Muslim Life does not collect:

  • Phone number or real name
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Camera or microphone data
  • Contacts or social data
  • Payment or financial information
  • Browsing history or analytics

🗃️ Data Retention

We retain your account data as long as your account is active. When you delete your account:

  • Your email and User ID are permanently deleted from Firebase Auth
  • All synced favorites and bookmarks are permanently deleted from Firestore
  • Deletion is processed within 30 days of your request
✅ No data is retained after account deletion.

🗑️ Account Deletion

You have the right to delete your account and all associated data at any time.

Option 1 — Delete inside the app:

  • Open Muslim Life → Go to Profile / Settings
  • Tap "Delete Account"
  • Confirm deletion — your account and all data are permanently removed

Option 2 — Request via email:

  • Email us at siratalmustaqeem60177@gmail.com
  • Subject: "Account Deletion Request – Muslim Life"
  • Include your registered email address
  • We will process your request within 30 days

🔑 Permissions Used

  • ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION — For precise prayer times and Qibla direction
  • ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION — Fallback location for prayer time calculation
  • INTERNET — For account sync and Firebase services

Revoke any permission anytime via Settings → Apps → Muslim Life → Permissions.

👶 Children's Privacy

Muslim Life is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe your child has created an account, please contact us to delete it immediately.
